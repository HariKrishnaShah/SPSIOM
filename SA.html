Speed & Agility Testing

(electronic timing gates)


As an athlete, speed and agility plays a huge role in most sports. The ability to go past an opposing player or beat them to the ball can be the difference between winning and losing. Generally, if an athletes speed and agility increases, so does their performance.

We can help you improve these qualities, but we can only truly know if you are improving by testing you. We are the only company on the Isle of Man that uses electronic timing gates to accurately test speed and agility.

We can use a variety of tests that are suitable to you and your sport. Please allow 1 hour for testing in which a sufficient warm up will be conducted prior. Team testing may take longer.

To learn more about individual or team speed and agility testing, please e-mail performance@spsiom.com.
